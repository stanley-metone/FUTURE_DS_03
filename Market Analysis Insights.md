# Nykaa Marketing Funnel Analysis

## Dataset Overview
- Campaign records analyzed: **55,555**
- Total impressions: **3,060,407,471**
- Total clicks: **260,445,757**
- Total leads: **104,291,797**
- Total customers/conversions: **57,380,922**
- Total revenue: **$28.66B**
- Average engagement score: **13.78**

## Funnel Conversion Performance

| Funnel Stage | Conversion Rate | Drop-off |
|---|---:|---:|
| Impressions → Clicks | 8.51% | 91.5% |
| Clicks → Leads | 40.04% | 60.0% |
| Leads → Customers | 55.02% | 45.0% |
| Overall Impression → Customer | 1.87% | — |

## Key Insights
1. **Biggest drop-off is at the awareness stage.** The funnel loses **2,799,961,714** users between impressions and clicks, equal to a **91.5%** drop-off.
2. **Lead nurturing performs better than the top funnel.** Lead-to-customer conversion is **55.02%**, showing stronger lower-funnel quality.
3. **Instagram leads by conversion volume**, with **19,226,869** attributed conversions.
4. **Instagram has the strongest lead-to-customer efficiency**, with a conversion rate of **55.26%**.
5. **Working Women is the strongest segment by customer volume**, producing **11,638,803** conversions.

## Opportunities to Improve Lead-to-Customer Conversion
- Improve top-funnel creative quality because impressions-to-clicks has the largest drop-off.
- A/B test hooks, offers, and CTAs to improve CTR before increasing spend.
- Retarget non-converting leads with personalized beauty bundles and first-purchase incentives.
- Prioritize high-performing customer segments like **Working Women**.
- Replicate the messaging and landing journey from **Instagram**, the highest-efficiency channel.
- Monitor CTR, click-to-lead, and lead-to-customer conversion weekly as the core funnel KPIs.
